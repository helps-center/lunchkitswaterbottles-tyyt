About.

Hey there!

We're all about making your meals and drinks easy, healthy, and good for the planet. We want to change how you carry, eat, and drink on the go. We make awesome lunch boxes and water bottles that are built to last, good for you, and easy on the earth.

Life can be wild! Whether you're a student, a worker, a traveler, or a busy parent, you need gear that can keep up. Our stuff is made to be just that – dependable, flexible, and super easy to use. We're trying to make it easier to live a healthy, green life. Every lunch and every drink is a chance to do something good for yourself and the world.

What we're trying to do

Our goal is easy: make everyday meals and staying hydrated simple, fun, and sustainable. We think the containers and bottles you use every day can totally change your habits, health, and how you treat the planet. We put a lot of care into making cool, safe, and inventive products. We hope this helps you live and eat better and make smarter choices.

We're serious about making products that are useful, tough, and look good. We want everyone to chill out and live in the moment with no stress. We offer cool products without losing good quality or hurting the planet. We hope to nudge people toward eating better, getting organized, and caring for the Earth.

What we sell

Lunch Boxes

Our lunch boxes are made for real life. They're not just containers; they're tools to help you eat well and stay organized.

*   **Sections Inside**: Keep different foods separate so they stay fresh. Great for meals, snacks, or even lunches with a few courses.
*   **Keep Hot or Cold**: They can keep your food at just the right temperature, which is great for long days at school, work, or when traveling.
*   **Strong Stuff**: Made from steel, great plastics, and other safe materials that can handle everyday use.
*   **Easy to Carry**: Light, won't leak, and comfy to carry, with handles or straps.
*   **Planet-Friendly**: Reusable and made to last a long time, so you don't have to use disposable stuff.

Our lunch boxes fit every kind of life – from kids' school lunches to office eats to trail snacks. They're useful, safe, and stylish, making your meals better than ever.

Water Bottles

You've gotta stay hydrated! Our water bottles make it easy, safe, and fun.

*   **Hot or Cold**: Keep your drinks just how you like them for hours. Fab for coffee, tea, or just keeping your water crisp!
*   **Easy to Carry**: Toss them in your backpack or bag and go.
*   **Safe and Strong**: Made from steel, safe plastics, and glass, so no gross stuff gets into your drinks.
*   **Easy to Use**: Wide openings for adding ice or cleaning, plus lids that flip open and shapes that feel great in your hand.
*   **Go Green**: Use these instead of plastic bottles to help save the planet.

Every water bottle is designed to last, help you drink more water, and cut down on plastic waste.
